«Revenue Authority of Sidama National Regional State was established to fasten the regional social and economic development, built tax system of reliable base, invulnerable for corruption risks and that offer fast and good service for regional society and wish to see tax payer society that pay tax in his motivation. To foster the social and the economic development and building good governance in the region it is necessary to produce a human resource having adequate professional knowledge and skill in the information and communication technology that enables to further strengthen the tax administration system using technology. By realizing joint and integrated working system with the Federal ministry of Revenue & Customs it has been found necessary to create one economic society which depends upon knowledge and information for the overall development acceleration and good governance. »
